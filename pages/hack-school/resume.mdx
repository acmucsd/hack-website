# Building a Resume

Creating a strong resume is an important step in your professional journey. Here are some quick tips and resources to help you get started:

## What to Include

- **Contact Information:** Name, email, LinkedIn, and phone number.
- **Education:** School, degree, graduation year.
- **Experience:** Jobs, internships, or relevant projects. Use bullet points to describe your impact.
- **Skills:** Programming languages, frameworks, tools, and soft skills.
- **Projects:** Highlight personal or class projects that show your abilities.
- **Leadership & Activities:** Clubs, organizations, or volunteer work.

## Tips

- Keep it to one page.
- Use clear, concise language.
- Focus on results and impact (use numbers when possible).
- Tailor your resume for each application, use relevant experiences.
- Use a clean resume format that makes the most of your space.
